About me

I’m a Product Designer with over 15 years of experience and a bachelor’s degree in Psychology. I love applying behavioural insight to design to help create products people love. I have front-end development experience spanning HTML, CSS and JavaScript, as well as working knowledge in React, Git/GitHub and Cursor IDE (it's how I made this website!). I create code-based prototypes that bridge design thinking and technical execution.
